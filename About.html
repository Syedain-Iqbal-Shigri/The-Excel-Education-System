Who We Are

Welcome to
Excel Public School

Since our founding in 2013, Excel Public School has been a beacon of academic excellence in Shigar, Baltistan. We believe that education is not merely the learning of facts, but the training of the mind to think, analyze, and create.

"Character is the foundation upon which all excellence is built."

Our campus provides a nurturing environment where students are encouraged to explore their potential through rigorous academics, competitive sports, and artistic expression — producing graduates who are confident, capable, and ready for the world.

Our Journey

A Decade of Milestones

From a humble beginning to a pillar of education in Baltistan.

2013

The Foundation

Excel Public School was founded with a class of 80 students and a vision to bring quality education to Shigar Valley.

2015

First Board Results

Our inaugural matriculation class achieved a 100% pass rate, establishing our reputation for academic excellence.

2018

Campus Expansion

A new science block, library, and sports complex were inaugurated, tripling the school's capacity to 600 students.

2021

Technology Integration

State-of-the-art computer labs and a robotics program were launched, bringing STEM education to Baltistan.

2024

1,200 Students Strong

Excel Public School now serves over 1,200 students, with 98% pass rates and a legacy of shaping future leaders.

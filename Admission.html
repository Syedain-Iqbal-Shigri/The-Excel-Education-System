Frequently Asked Questions

What is the age requirement for Grade 1?
Students must be 5 years old by August 31st of the enrollment year to be eligible for Grade 1.
Do you offer financial aid or scholarships?
Yes, we offer a comprehensive financial aid program based on family need. Additionally, we offer merit-based scholarships for high-achieving students.
Is there an entrance exam?
Yes, for entry into Middle and High School, students are required to take a placement test in English and Math to ensure appropriate academic placement.
Can international students apply?
Absolutely. We welcome international students and provide support for visa processing, English language learning, and host family arrangements.
